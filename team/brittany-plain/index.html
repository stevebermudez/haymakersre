Brittany brings a decade of experience to Haymakers. She has a comprehensive background in commercial and residential real estate, coupled with strong client relationship skills and a proven ability to navigate complex transactions effectively. Her career has been built on her ability to structure creative solutions, execute strategies, and her sheer determination to deliver optimal outcomes for her clients. With strong negotiation skills and a deep understanding of commercial real estate processes, Brittany consistently adds value to each undertaking.

Brittany is passionate about empowering business and real estate entrepreneurs to achieve their objectives. Whether facilitating acquisitions, educating clients on changing market conditions, or guiding them through complex processes such as entitlement, zoning and compliance, Brittany is relentless in her pursuit of her clients' success. She approaches every project with a focus on achieving measurable results and exceeding expectations. Her technical expertise and strategic approach make her a trusted partner for clients navigating the complexities of the commercial real estate landscape.

Brittany holds a degree in Business Administration and Marketing from San Diego State University and is licensed to practice real estate in California and Wisconsin.
